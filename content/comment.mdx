## Introduction

At Blazed Labs, we encourage open dialogue and value the contributions of our community. To ensure a respectful and constructive environment, we have established the following guidelines for comments and discussions:

1. **Respectful Communication**
We expect all users to communicate respectfully. Personal attacks, harassment, or hate speech of any kind will not be tolerated.
2. **Relevance**
Comments should be relevant to the topic at hand. Off-topic comments may be removed to maintain the focus of discussions.
3. **No Spam or Promotions**
Unsolicited advertisements, promotional content, or spam will be deleted. Please refrain from posting links to unrelated websites or services.
4. **Intellectual Property**
Ensure that you have the right to share any content (text, images, etc.) you post. Do not infringe on the intellectual property rights of others.
5. **Privacy and Safety**
Do not share personal information (yours or others') in comments. Respect the privacy of fellow users. Please refer to our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for more information.
6. **Moderation and Enforcement**
Our moderation team reserves the right to review and remove any comments that violate these guidelines. Repeated violations may result in account suspension or banning.
7. **Reporting Violations**
If you see a comment that violates our policy, please report it to our moderation team using the provided reporting mechanism.

By participating in discussions on Blazed Labs, Blazed One, and/or any other Blazed Company Websites, you agree to abide by this Comment Policy. 

***Thank you for helping us maintain a positive and welcoming community!***

## Changes to this Comment Policy

We may update this Comment Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the updated Comment Policy on our website, and the revised policy will be effective as of the updated *"Last Edited Date"* stated at the beginning of this policy.
